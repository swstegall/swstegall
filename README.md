### 👨‍💻 About me

I'm a mobile developer who builds for **Android (Kotlin)** and **iOS (Swift)** by day, and dabbles in just about everything else by night — Rust, C/C++, TypeScript, and a healthy collection of emulators, game engines, and CLI tools. I love shipping things, learning new stacks, and the occasional weekend hackathon.

When I'm not writing apps, I'm usually reverse-engineering and reviving an old game I love. ⚔️

### 🐉 Final Fantasy XIV hobby projects

A big chunk of my free time goes toward preserving **FINAL FANTASY XIV v1.23b** — the original 2010 1.0 release, *before* A Realm Reborn. It's a piece of MMO history that the world otherwise lost, and bringing it back to life is my favorite playground.

- ⚙️ **[Garlemald-Server](https://github.com/swstegall/Garlemald-Server)** — A Rust port of Project Meteor Server: a private-server emulator for FFXIV 1.0. Lobby, world, map, and web services in a single Cargo workspace, backed by SQLite and driving 1,142 upstream Lua content scripts.
- 🚀 **[Garlemald-Client](https://github.com/swstegall/Garlemald-Client)** — A cross-platform Rust launcher for FFXIV v1.23b private servers (macOS / Linux / Windows).
- 🔬 **[meteor-decomp](https://github.com/swstegall/meteor-decomp)** & **[decomp-agents](https://github.com/swstegall/decomp-agents)** — Decompilation tooling for digging into the original client.
- 🍎 **[XIV-1.0-Apple-Silicon-Installer](https://github.com/swstegall/XIV-1.0-Apple-Silicon-Installer)** — Getting the 1.0 client running on Apple Silicon.
